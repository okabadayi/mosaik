## Target problem

`<business>` faces operational complexity across multiple departments, each with manual workflows that AI could absorb. The challenge is doing this WHILE maintaining a unified view of the business so cross-domain insights aren't lost.

## Our approach

Treat `<business>`'s AI transformation as a coordinated portfolio of solutions, each owned by a per-solution repo, all anchored to this meta-repo's unified strategy. One agent with whole-business context emits per-problem solutions while preserving the unified picture.

## Who it's for

**Primary**: `<business>`'s operator-architect (the person responsible for AI transformation).
**Secondary**: per-department stakeholders whose workflows get absorbed by solutions.

## Key metrics

- Number of solutions shipped via the methodology
- Time-to-first-solution for new identified pain points
- Cross-solution pattern reuse (evidence of compounding)

## Tracks

### T1. Per-solution delivery
Acute solutions to individual departmental pain points. Each ships via its own per-solution repo following CE's per-feature discipline.

### T2. Cross-solution compounding
Patterns identified across solutions accumulate in `solutions/` (this repo) at the right abstraction layer.

### T3. Unified context maintenance
STRATEGY.md, projects/, decisions/, and people knowledge stay current so the unified agent always has fresh business context.
